Tension

Matters of global tension between superpowers is more relevant now than ever, as unfortunate that may be. Russia has recently increased their DEFCON level, in light of their invasion of Ukraine and how NATO countries have responded. Of the nine countries in possession of nuclear weapons, conflict between the US and their allies (the UK, France) and Russia and their allies (China informally, and as an extension North Korea) would be quite problematic, to say the least.
